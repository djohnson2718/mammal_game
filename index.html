  • The taxonomy of mammals is constantly being revaluated as research continues, but in 2020 Wikipedia listed 28 Orders of mammals.

    • How many mammals of different Orders can you name?

  • Type the name of any mammal in the box at the top and press Enter or click Submit.
  • If it is from an Order you haven't found yet, you get 1 point!
  • If it is from an Order you've already used, you get a small penalty of 0.2 points.
  • If the animal is not in the database (or you spelled it wrong), there is no penalty.
  • When you can't think of any more, click the button to finalize your score and see the answers!

    • Tips

  • The database prefers general names. For example, if this was the amphibian game, "Pyrenean brook newt" would not be in the database, but "newt" would be.
  • Of course, if those extra words change which Order the animal belongs to, the long name will be included.
  • For your enjoyment, each Order has a representative picture. It might not be the same animal you typed in.
  • Don't use the browser's Back button or you will lose your progress. Scroll or use the menu buttons in the top right to navigate the page.
  • 6 or 7 of the Orders are pretty obscure. To help you out, I filled "monito del monte" into the input box for you!
